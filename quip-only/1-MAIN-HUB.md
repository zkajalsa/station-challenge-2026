# ⚽ Station Challenge — FIFA World Cup 2026

## Predict. Compete. Win.

Welcome to the Station Challenge! Predict match scores for the FIFA World Cup 2026 and compete against your fellow associates for the top spot on the leaderboard.

---

## 📋 How to Participate

1. Open the **Prediction Sheet** (link below)
2. Find the match you want to predict
3. Add a NEW ROW with your details:
   - Your **Amazon Login**
   - Your **Full Name**
   - Your **Shift** (night / early / late)
   - The **Match Code** (e.g. GS-A1)
   - Your predicted score: **Score A** and **Score B**
4. Done! You can predict as many matches as you want
5. Predictions LOCK at kickoff — no changes after that

## 📜 Rules

1. **One prediction per match per person** — if you have duplicate rows, only the first one counts
2. **Use your real Amazon login** — QUIP tracks who edits what, so we'll verify
3. **Predictions lock at kickoff** — any edits after the match starts will be disqualified
4. **Don't edit other people's rows** — QUIP revision history shows everything
5. **Tiebreaker** — if two people have the same points, the one with more exact score predictions wins. If still tied, the one who predicted more matches wins.
